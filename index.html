The Pan African Mathematics Olympiad (PAMO) is an African Championship Mathematics Competition for High School students and is held annually. PAMO is an activity of the African Mathematical Union Commission on Pan African Mathematics Olympiads (AMUPAMOC) created in 1986, one year later, after the creation of African Mathematical Union (AMU) in Morocco in 1976.

African Mathematical Union was Founded in Rabat, in July 1976, during the first Pan African Congress of Mathematicians. Its creation had been prepared during several meetings of African mathematicians that took place during the early 1970s in and outside Africa. https://www.africamathunion.org/AMU-pamo-official.php, which is currently the official website of PAMO.
The theme and design of the website is based on iph-unofficial.org which is also based on imo-official.org.

PAMO-unofficial.org is an open source project. View on Github.
